# RC-SBDD-Bench-v1 Metrics

Submissions are ranked primarily by target-level downstream success and
calibrated reliability:

- dock-fast success;
- mean calibrated risk;
- high-risk fraction;
- QED preservation;
- coverage under selective-risk thresholds;
- optional GNINA/Vina, PoseBusters, and AiZynthFinder audit fields.

The official paper analysis uses target-level paired tests rather than pooled
molecule-level tests.
